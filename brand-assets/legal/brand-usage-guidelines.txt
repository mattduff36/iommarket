iTrader.im Brand Usage Guidelines
========================================

1. Always use official logo files from this package.
2. Maintain minimum clear space around the logo.
3. Do not alter colors, proportions, or effects.
4. Use dark variants on dark backgrounds, light variants on light backgrounds.
5. The vortex icon may be used standalone at sizes >= 32px.
6. Primary brand colors: Red #E22229, Blue #157BCA, Gold #C48551
7. For questions, contact the brand team.
